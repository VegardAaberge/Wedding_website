- About Us -

“Love drives me to Norway, a pretty country full of nature. So fortunate of me to have this meaningful job, a job to create happiness for wedding couples. All brides are entitled to be able to choose the wedding dresses that suit them best without price worries,” Said Meng,owner of Aaberge Brudesalong.

Aaberge Brudesalong provides high quality wedding dresses for all preferences. Our designers follow the wedding dress trend tightly and design wedding dresses suit the need of Norwegians. We have a longterm cooperation with a manufaturing center in Su Zhou,China. Su zhou is a city with over 2500 years history and well kown for its silk products and delicated textile manufacturing skills.

When you order a wedding dress from us, we will send your measurements to our factory. Tailors will then make the dress and send it back to us through DHL. Normal orders will take 60-90 days. For brides who are short in time, we also provide express order. By paying for express service, you can get your customised wedding dress within 45 days. We will contact you for fitting as soon as your dress arrive. Welcome to Aaberge Brudesalong to experience all we have to offer!
